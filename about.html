About Us

Our History

June 2016 -

The idea of a new institution run by a special education project group is formed at a cafe in Corona, California, USA. It would be called ProjectPlato.

July 2016 -

The idea turns into reality, and a number of groups volunteer to help build the institution. A group of teachers and former school officials form the ProjectPlato Union

December 2016-

The Institution's construction and renovation is complete, enrollments are open to the public.

January 2017 -

1,500 students from Riverside County to Orange county enroll into the institution for education.

June 2017-

Nearly all 1,500 students graduate in success, earning degress and diplomas. ProjectPlato is successful, drawing the attention of the Education department to fund the project.

Graduation Ceremony

Our graduation ceremonies are like any other institution. Our ceremonies give away diplomas and degress honor the student for their mastery in a certain subject.
